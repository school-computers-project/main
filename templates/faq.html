Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



What is go karting?


Go karting is a thrilling motorsport that involves driving small, open-wheel vehicles known as go-karts on specially designed tracks. It's a fun and competitive activity suitable for all ages.



How old do you have to be to go karting?


Generally, most go karting centers have age restrictions. Our facility welcomes drivers as young as 7 years old. Younger participants may be able to ride in tandem with an adult.


Do I need any prior experience to go karting?


No prior experience is necessary. Our go karts are designed for both beginners and experienced drivers. Our staff provides a safety briefing and basic instructions before each session.


What should I wear for go karting?


We recommend wearing comfortable clothing and closed-toe shoes. Helmets and other necessary safety gear will be provided by our facility. Long hair should be tied back, and loose clothing or scarves should be avoided.


Can I bring my own helmet?


While we provide helmets to ensure safety and compliance with regulations, you are welcome to bring your own helmet if it meets safety standards and is approved by our staff.


Are reservations required?


It's highly recommended to make a reservation, especially during peak hours or weekends, to ensure availability. Walk-ins are welcome, but availability may be limited.


How long is a typical go-karting session?


The duration of a go-karting session varies, but on average, each session lasts around 30 minutes. The actual time on the track may be affected by factors such as track conditions and group size.
