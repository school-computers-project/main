Velocikids

The premier go karting site.

Velocikids is the premier go karting site in the Lower Mainland aimed at children aged 7-17. We provide world-renowned instructors, state-of-the-art go karts, and award-winning indoor and outdoor tracks. Located in downtown Port Moody, we are the go-to site for all your speed needs!
